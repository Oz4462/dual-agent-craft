# Shift Note — template

## <YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC> — <topic>
- **DONE**: what got finished, WITH the verify evidence (command + result).
- **OPEN**: what is half-done; the exact next command/step to resume.
- **DECISIONS**: choices made + why (one line each).
- **LESSON**: what failed and what rule/muscle-memory it becomes ("none" is allowed, lying is not).
- **STATE**: branch, dirty files, suite status, spend if notable.
